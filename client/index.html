STARS Tutoring Schedule

Welcome to the STARS Tutoring Schedule! If you would like to see the hours covered for a course, set the filter at the top left to "Course". Then choose a course when prompted. You can hover over or press on a time block to see the tutors who cover shifts during that block and the hours they work. If you would like to see when a specific tutor is available, filter by "Tutor". Choose the name of the tutor you are looking for when prompted. You can see the courses that tutor covers by hovering over or pressing on the "Courses Covered" button.
